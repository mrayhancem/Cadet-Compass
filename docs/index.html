8–12 Roadmap Service Academies ROTC Enlistment Student Toolkit AI Coach

From Grade 8 to Graduation: one clear path to Service Academies, ROTC, Guard/Reserve, or Enlistment.

Cadet Compass is a counselor-built website and AI tool. It organizes military options into a single, student-friendly roadmap, then uses AI to personalize next steps, check readiness, and prevent missed deadlines.

Start the Student Toolkit Open the Digital Demo

Privacy: no student logins required. The AI Coach can run in “offline mode” (no data sent) or “live mode” (API-based) depending on school settings.

Quick start

  1. Open the 8–12 Roadmap.
  2. Run the Readiness Check.
  3. Use the Resume Builder.
  4. Ask the AI Coach questions.

What students get

  • Plain-language explanations of each pathway
  • Grade-by-grade timeline (8–12)
  • Checklists, templates, and summer program planner
  • AI Coach that personalizes next steps and alternatives

What counselors get

  • Standardized advising workflow (same language for every student)
  • Fast triage: academy-competitive vs ROTC-competitive vs enlistment-ready
  • Built-in responsible AI guardrails and citations
  • Exportable student action plan (print/email)

Responsible AI

The AI Coach is restricted to education/career guidance. It does not provide operational instructions or anything unsafe. It points students to official sources for requirements and emphasizes counselor/parent confirmation.
