Cadet Compass — Judge/Reviewer Demo Links

This page is designed to satisfy the Track III expectation of a “video or other electronic demonstration.” The primary demonstration is the live, interactive website (no login required).

Privacy note: The AI Coach is for general advising only. Do not enter student names, addresses, SSNs, medical details, or other sensitive personal information.

Live site (primary demo)

Open the live site and explore the pages and AI Coach workflow.

Live URL: https://cadetcompass.netlify.app/
AI Coach: https://cadetcompass.netlify.app/chat
Student Toolkit hub: https://cadetcompass.netlify.app/toolkit

Suggested 2-minute judge walkthrough

  1. Open Home and confirm it is a Grades 8–12 advising tool.
  2. Open 8–12 Roadmap to see grade-by-grade guidance.
  3. Open AI Coach and click a “quick prompt” chip (Plan A/B/C, Academy vs ROTC).
  4. Follow any “verify on official sources” links through the Toolkit.
Note: The site supports a safe “local fallback” if the AI function is unavailable. This ensures students can still receive structured guidance without external AI access.

Source code (transparency)

The project is open-source for review. The AI Coach is implemented as a Netlify Function and a front-end chat UI with a local knowledge fallback.
