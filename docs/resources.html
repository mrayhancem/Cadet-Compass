Student Toolkit (Texas-wide)

Use these templates to build a clean, verifiable student packet: resume, leadership log, interview prep, and official link hub. This page avoids fragile “hard-coded” requirements by linking to official sources for final verification.

What to build (one student packet)

1) Resume (1 page)

  • Leadership roles + dates
  • Service impact (not just hours)
  • Awards and honors
  • Work experience + responsibilities

2) Leadership log

  • Role
  • Hours
  • Measurable outcomes
  • Adult contact (optional)

3) Story bank

  • 3 leadership stories
  • 2 teamwork stories
  • 1 failure → recovery story
  • Why military / why officer (if applicable)

C) Interview question bank

          

INTERVIEW BANK (write bullet answers; practice out loud)

WHY
- Why do you want to serve?
- Why officer (if officer pathway)?
- Why this branch/program?

LEADERSHIP
- Tell me about a time you led under pressure.
- Tell me about a time you failed. What did you do next?
- Tell me about a time you handled conflict.

CHARACTER
- Tell me about a time you did the right thing when it was hard.
- What values guide your decisions?

TEAMWORK
- Tell me about a time you worked with someone difficult.
- Tell me about a time you helped a teammate succeed.

FITNESS / DISCIPLINE
- How do you stay consistent when you’re busy?
- What habits are you building now?

CLOSING
- What questions do you have for us?
