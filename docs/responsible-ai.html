Responsible AI

Cadet Compass uses AI to scale counseling-quality guidance while protecting student privacy and preventing misinformation.

What this AI does

  • Generates customized timelines and next steps based on grade level and pathway goals.
  • Summarizes options and points to official sources for verification.
  • Helps students structure resumes and interview stories without fabricating achievements.

What this AI does not do

  • It does not make final admissions decisions.
  • It does not request or store sensitive personal data.
  • It does not promise eligibility or benefits; it instructs verification via official sources.

Student privacy

This site is designed to work without login. When the optional AI API is enabled, only the chat message is sent to the AI endpoint; no student identifiers are required.
