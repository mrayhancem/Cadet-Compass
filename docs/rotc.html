ROTC scholarship readiness checklist

  • Academics: plan a rigorous schedule and keep grades strong.
  • Fitness: train consistently and document improvement.
  • Leadership: build evidence of initiative (captain roles, officer roles, projects).
  • Service and character: sustained community involvement and positive discipline history.
  • Interview readiness: practice a clear “why service” narrative.
Practical advantage: ROTC can be a strong Plan A or Plan B for students who want an officer career but prefer a traditional college experience.
