Enlisted Pathway

Enlistment, National Guard, and Reserves

Enlistment can provide training, pay, and education benefits. Many enlisted service members later commission via OCS/OTS or other programs (policies vary).

Branch entry points (official)

  • U.S. Army
  • U.S. Navy
  • U.S. Air Force
  • U.S. Marine Corps
  • U.S. Coast Guard
  • U.S. Space Force
Advisor note: Students should use official branch websites and meet recruiters with a parent/guardian when appropriate.

AI-assisted “fit” questions

Use the AI Coach page to answer these accurately and build a plan:

  1. What careers interest you (cyber, aviation, medical, mechanical, logistics)?
  2. Do you want full-time active duty or part-time Guard/Reserve?
  3. Do you want to commission as an officer eventually?
  4. What education goal do you have (certificate, associate, bachelor’s, graduate)?

Education and benefits (verification required)

Education benefits and state programs can change. Cadet Compass intentionally avoids publishing dollar amounts or promises. Use the Toolkit’s official links and verification checklist before making commitments.
