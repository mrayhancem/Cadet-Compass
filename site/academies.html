Academy readiness checklist (non-negotiables)

  1. Academic rigor + strong grades (verify current expectations on each official site).
  2. Leadership roles and sustained involvement.
  3. Physical readiness (train consistently; prepare for academy fitness assessments).
  4. Strong character + references; interview readiness.
  5. Medical qualification process (DoDMERB or academy-specific medical process).
  6. Nomination requirements for academies that require them (verify by academy).
Cadet Compass approach: build a primary plan + backup plan (ROTC) + safety plan (enlist then commission later).

Texas student notes

  • Start the nomination conversation in Grade 10 and execute in Grade 11. Confirm current offices and processes via official congressional websites.
  • Use Cadet Compass Toolkit to build a leadership resume and interview story bank.
  • Plan SAT/ACT (and fee waivers if eligible) early; confirm whether each academy uses test scores in the current cycle.
Accuracy rule: This site does not publish numeric cutoffs because they change. Always verify from official admissions pages.
